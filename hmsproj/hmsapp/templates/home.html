    Frequently Asked Questions

    Find answers to common questions about our medical services, appointments, insurance coverage, and patient care procedures at Medicio. Our commitment is to provide clear, helpful information to our patients.

    What are your visiting hours and policies?

    General visiting hours are from 10:00 AM to 8:00 PM daily. ICU and special care units have specific visiting hours (2:00 PM - 4:00 PM and 6:00 PM - 7:00 PM). We request visitors to maintain silence and follow hospital guidelines. Due to health safety protocols, children under 12 may have restricted access to certain areas.

    How do I schedule an appointment with a specialist?

    You can schedule appointments through our online booking system, by calling our appointment desk at (555) 123-4567, or visiting our reception desk. For specialist consultations, you may need a referral from your primary care physician. Emergency cases are handled immediately without prior appointments.

    What insurance plans do you accept?

    We accept most major insurance plans including Medicare, Blue Cross Blue Shield, Aetna, Cigna, and UnitedHealthcare. Please contact our billing department or check with your insurance provider about specific coverage details. We also offer flexible payment plans for uninsured patients.

    What should I bring for hospital admission?

    Please bring your photo ID, insurance card, list of current medications, relevant medical records, comfortable clothing, and basic toiletries. For planned admissions, our admission desk will provide you with a detailed checklist during pre-admission counseling.

    Do you have emergency services available 24/7?

    Yes, our emergency department is open 24/7 with fully equipped trauma care facilities. We have specialized emergency medical teams available round the clock, including critical care specialists, surgeons, and support staff. Ambulance services are also available 24/7.

    How can I access my medical records?

    You can access your medical records through our patient portal or by submitting a written request to our Medical Records Department. For privacy and security reasons, we require proper identification and may take up to 7-10 working days to process physical copy requests.
